1. The Problem

REACH Compliance

Chemical SMEs are drowning in regulation

REACH (EC 1907/2006) requires registration of every chemical substance manufactured or imported into the EU above 1 tonne/year. Compliance costs €50K-€500K per substance. The process involves hazard classification (CLP), exposure modeling, Chemical Safety Reports, Safety Data Sheets, and IUCLID dossier preparation.

Most SMEs use manual processes or expensive consultants. Legacy software (IUCLID, Chesar) is free but painfully complex. There's no AI-native solution that automates the heavy lifting.

H2 Intelligence

Hydrogen projects fly blind on catalyst selection

The EU hydrogen economy is scaling fast (REPowerEU, IPCEI). Project developers must choose catalysts, predict performance, model economics, and navigate subsidy eligibility — often with incomplete data and expensive consultants.

No platform exists that combines catalyst chemistry knowledge with techno-economic modeling and EU funding intelligence. Decisions worth millions are made on spreadsheets.

2. The Solution

One platform. Two intelligence modules. Shared AI engine.

Three Worlds Chemical Intelligence is a B2B SaaS platform that uses AI (LLMs + domain-specific ML models) to automate chemical compliance and hydrogen project assessment. Built by a PhD chemist who understands the science, not just the software.

Module 1 — REACH Engine

AI-Powered Chemical Compliance

Substance Identification & Grouping: Upload chemical data → AI identifies substances, groups analogues, and suggests read-across strategies.

Hazard Classification (CLP): AI predicts GHS classifications using QSAR models trained on ECHA's registered substances database. Reduces manual toxicology assessment by 70%.

Safety Data Sheet Generation: Automated SDS creation in all EU languages, compliant with Annex II format. AI fills sections from chemical properties and regulatory data.

Dossier Preparation: Pre-fills IUCLID format dossiers. AI generates robust study summaries, exposure scenarios, and Chemical Safety Reports.

Regulatory Monitoring: Real-time alerts on SVHC candidate list updates, restriction proposals, and authorization deadlines.

Module 2 — H2 Intelligence

Green Hydrogen Project Assessment

Catalyst Selection Engine: AI recommends optimal catalyst formulations (Ru, Ni, Fe-based) for ammonia cracking, water electrolysis, and steam reforming based on project parameters.

Performance Prediction: ML models trained on published catalysis literature predict conversion rates, selectivity, degradation curves, and operational lifetimes.

Techno-Economic Modeling: Input project specs → get CAPEX, OPEX, LCOH (Levelized Cost of Hydrogen), IRR, and payback period projections.

EU Funding Navigator: Maps project characteristics to eligible funding programs (Innovation Fund, IPCEI, REPowerEU, Horizon Europe). Generates eligibility scorecards.

Due Diligence Reports: One-click generation of investor-ready technical feasibility reports for hydrogen projects.
